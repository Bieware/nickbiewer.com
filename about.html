Who I Am

I am Graphic Artist with experience working in various fields of design. I recieved my BFA with degree in Digital Art in 2010 from The College of New Jersey.

I have experience working in the marketing departments of fast-pased advertising and finance companies, creating print and digital collateral, as well as building and distributing HTML email campaigns. I am able to indiviually take lead on projects, while also working with other graphic design and marketing teams to fulfill any request.

My freelance work consists of logos, banner ads, album designs and more for businesses and local musicians in order to captivate their viewers, build their brand and promote interest in their product.
